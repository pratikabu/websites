Brief

It is a very simple extension to provide a mechanism to change the title of any tab, in addition to that it gives you features to toggle the title and icon visibility on/off.

This project is still under development phase, as of now it is available only for Chrome browser with few functionalities. Plan is to make it fully functional and available for Firefox, Safari and Opera as well. Your reviews and feedback are most welcome. If you wish you can donate for its progress. It will help.

If you want to see more of my development you can navigate to My Work page. Scroll to Top is one of the very popular extension in my portfolio.
