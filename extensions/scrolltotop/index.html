Scroll To Top

Scrolling on pages is really a tiring experience. And now a days there are sites which provides infinite scrolling (like facebook, twitter, Google+, etc). You will keep scrolling to see more and more updates and what if you want to go up to the top of the page. Will you scroll the scroll bar to top? With this add-on scrolling will be more fun than just scroll. It automatically adds an image at top right corner of page to give you one click scrolling to top with beautiful animation.

Moreover it is available for your browser. Choose your favorite browser.
